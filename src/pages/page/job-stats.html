Key Insights

  • 1
    Experience Paradox: 32.5% of jobs require 1-3 years experience, but only 5.6% accept fresh graduates
  • 2
    Technical Skills: Excel (38%) and ERP systems (42%) are non-negotiable requirements
  • 3
    Remote Work: Only 1% offer fully remote positions; 95.6% require on-site presence
  • 4
    Salary Gap: Management roles earn 70%+ premium over entry-level (IDR 12.5M vs 7.25M median)
  • 5
    Transparency: 62% of employers don't disclose salary information in job postings
  • 6
    Education: 53% require Bachelor's degree (S1), only 6.6% accept high school graduates
